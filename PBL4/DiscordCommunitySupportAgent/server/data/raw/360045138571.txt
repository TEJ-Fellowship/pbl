What Is Discord?

Discord is a communication platform that allows users to interact through text, voice, and video. Users can join or create their own communities, which can be organized around various topics, interests, or gaming groups.

What Are Text And Voice Channels?¬†

Your server is made up of text channels and voice channels. This allows you to communicate with the other members of the server.

Text Channels

Text channels provide dedicated spaces for written conversations, helping to keep discussions organized and ensuring everyone has room to participate. You can create individual channels for each topic your group enjoys discussing‚Äîwhether that's planning fishing trips, sharing cooking tips, or any other shared interests.

Voice Channels

Voice channels allow you to connect with others through voice and video chat. Simply click on a voice channel to join‚Äîno calling or ringing necessary. Other members of your server can see when you're in a voice channel and easily drop in to chat, say hello on video, or share their screen with the group.
